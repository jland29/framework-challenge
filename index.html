Lansingville Coffee Roasters

About Us

We started roasting coffee 8 years ago with the goal of creating the best quality coffee in the entire region!

While we have been in operation, we have expanded our variety of beans to include more interesting flavors, support local and international businesses, and our menu to better serve those with different tastes!

Our beans are ground right here in-store to maximize the freshness and quality of what we serve you, whether it be a prepared drink, or a bag of coffee grounds. Your next cup of coffee is sure to be very delicious!

In addition to our freshly ground coffee beans, we offer a variety of specialty drinks using our very own recipes to maximize the quality. You can say goodbye to Starbucks after your first sip! We offer a variety of customization options for our drinks, so be sure to find the perfect combination!
